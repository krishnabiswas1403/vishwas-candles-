Our Story

Crafted from
the Heart

Vishwas Candles was born out of a simple idea — that light has the power to transform any space into a sanctuary. We hand-pour every candle using 100% natural soy and beeswax, blended with premium fragrance oils and dried botanicals.

From our small workshop in India, each batch is made in limited quantities to ensure every candle you receive carries the warmth and intention it was made with.
